**🚖 Ride Analytics Dashboard: Ola Booking Insights**

A dynamic, interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze ride-booking operations—focusing on cancellations, revenue trends, vehicle performance, and customer behavior.

• Project Overview

Ride-sharing platforms like Ola operate at massive scale, handling thousands of bookings daily across different cities and vehicle categories.

While the demand for rides is consistently high, operational challenges such as frequent cancellations, inconsistent service quality, and revenue leakage significantly impact overall business performance.

This project simulates a real-world business scenario where raw booking data is transformed into a structured analytics solution to uncover inefficiencies and support decision-making.


• Objective

The objective of this project is to build an end-to-end analytics dashboard that helps stakeholders understand booking performance, identify operational bottlenecks, and improve overall efficiency.

The project focuses on:
Monitoring booking trends and ride volume
Identifying the root causes of ride cancellations
Analyzing revenue generation and payment behavior
Understanding vehicle usage patterns
Highlighting high-value customers for retention strategies


• Tech Stack

The project was developed using a combination of data processing, analysis, and visualization tools:

• 📊 Power BI was used to build interactive dashboards and visual reports
• 📂 Microsoft Excel was used for initial data cleaning and preprocessing
• 🧠 MySQL was used for querying, data validation, and creating reusable views
• 📝 DAX was used to calculate key metrics such as cancellation rate and total revenue
• 🔗 Data modeling was applied to establish relationships and enable cross-filtering across visuals


• Data Source

The dataset used in this project is a simulated ride-booking dataset inspired by platforms such as Ola and Uber.

It consists of approximately 100,000 records with 19 columns, including booking details such as booking status, vehicle type, ride distance, payment method, and customer and driver ratings.

The dataset represents realistic operational scenarios, making it suitable for performing business analysis and building decision-support dashboards.

• Features / Highlights
• Business Problem

Ride-sharing platforms operate at massive scale, handling thousands of bookings daily. While demand is high, a significant portion of rides fail due to cancellations, directly impacting revenue and customer satisfaction.

However, stakeholders often lack a unified view to understand operational inefficiencies. Raw data alone does not clearly explain where the problem lies—whether it is due to driver availability, customer behavior, or service reliability.

Key questions such as:
Which factors contribute most to ride cancellations?
Are drivers or customers responsible for the majority of failed bookings?
How do different vehicle types perform across ride distances?
Which payment methods drive the highest revenue?

…are difficult to answer quickly without a structured analytical solution.


• Goal of the Dashboard

To develop an interactive Power BI dashboard that provides a comprehensive view of ride-booking operations and enables stakeholders to make informed decisions.

The dashboard is designed to:
Enable users to monitor booking trends and overall platform performance
Identify and analyze the root causes of ride cancellations
Understand customer behavior and high-value segments
Explore vehicle utilization patterns across different ride categories
Support operational improvements through data-driven insights


• Walkthrough of Key Visuals

Key KPIs (Top Section)
Displays core metrics such as total bookings, total revenue generated from successful rides (~₹34M), and overall cancellation rate (~28%), giving an instant snapshot of business performance.

Booking Trends Over Time (Line Chart)
Visualizes ride volume across different dates, helping identify demand patterns, peak booking periods, and fluctuations in usage.

Booking Status Breakdown (Pie Chart)
Shows the proportion of successful vs cancelled bookings, allowing users to quickly assess the scale of operational inefficiency.

Cancellation Analysis (Dual Pie Charts)
Breaks down cancellations into customer and driver categories, highlighting the dominant reasons behind ride failures and enabling deeper root-cause analysis.

Revenue by Payment Method (Bar Chart)
Compares revenue generated through UPI, cash, and card transactions, helping understand customer payment preferences and digital adoption trends.

Vehicle Type Performance (Cards & Metrics)
Displays metrics such as average ride distance and total booking value by vehicle type, revealing how different categories serve short-distance vs long-distance demand.

Top Customers (Table Visual)
Ranks the top 5 customers based on booking value, enabling identification of high-value users for potential loyalty or retention strategies.


• Business Impact & Insights

Operational Efficiency: The dashboard highlights a high cancellation rate (~28%), indicating a major gap in service reliability that directly affects revenue.

Root Cause Identification: Analysis shows that driver-related issues contribute significantly to cancellations, pointing toward the need for better driver management and support systems.

Fleet Optimization: Clear differences in ride distance across vehicle types provide insights for better fleet allocation and pricing strategies.

Customer Strategy: Identification of top customers enables targeted engagement and loyalty programs to improve retention.

Revenue Understanding: Payment analysis reveals strong adoption of digital methods like UPI, helping businesses optimize payment systems and cash flow management.

Overall, this dashboard transforms fragmented booking data into a structured decision-making tool, enabling stakeholders to move from reactive problem-solving to proactive business optimization.


• Conclusion

This project demonstrates how raw booking data can be transformed into meaningful business insights using a structured analytics approach.

By integrating data cleaning, SQL-based analysis, and interactive visualization, the dashboard provides a complete view of operations and enables better, data-driven decision-making in a ride-sharing business context.
